Vex Scout

An app to help other for scouting

Vex Scout is a brand new app designed to bring all the tools of vex scouting to your fingertips. Vex Scout capitalizes on pre- scouting. The app allows the user to search through scores, averages, teams, competitions, awards, and much more. Vex Scout uses up-to-date statistics, making sure only accurate data is received. With Vex Scout anyone can sharpen their scouting skills and take their team to the next level. Features: “MY TEAM” Vex Scout allows for the user to “bookmark” their own team to make sure their team is keeping up with the competition. SKILLS: Use Vex Scout to check up on the top 50 Robot Skills and Programming Skills rankings across the world. TEAM PROFILES: Vex Scout allows the user to search through every team in the world to find up-to-date and detailed statistics on each team. COMPETITIONS: Users can also link to competitions from team profiles to see rankings, competition attendees, awards, and competition based statistics.
